<h3 align="left">👨‍💻 About Me</h3>

I'm a self-taught Software Engineer from Brazil 🇧🇷, now based in Vancouver, Canada 🇨🇦 — combining 10 years of design & marketing instinct with 5+ years of TypeScript engineering.

Previously on the **AlgoKit team at Algorand Foundation**, where I shipped developer tooling, CI/CD pipelines, and worked across 20+ repos before the March 2026 restructuring. Before that, I built **CompX Labs** (Algorand DeFi), shipped a $70k shuffle system at **Dartroom** in 3 days, and grew **Algogator** to 300% user expansion.

I got my start the unconventional way — invited to a castle hackathon in Spain 🏰, I built a DAO toolkit that caught the Algorand Foundation's attention. David Rojas created an internship path for me, and that became a full engineering career. No CS degree, just relentless building and a refusal to stop learning.

Today I'm focused on **AI agent systems** and **open-source tooling** — building autonomous workflows with OpenClaw, multi-agent pipelines, and MCP integration. I believe the future of software is agents that actually ship.
